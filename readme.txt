== Description ==

This plugin adds a spot to the bottom of all single posts with social share icons for Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Linkedin and Pinterest. It also adjusts to keep those social icons stuck on the bottom of the screen when readers are viewing your posts on mobile phones, which will allow users to share your posts from their phones easily.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'GuerrillaSharing folder' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory or add it directly in your WordPress admin panel
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
